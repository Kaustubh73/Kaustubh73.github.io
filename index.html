About

I am a Data Scientist at Oracle working on large language models. My research interests lie at the intersection of mechanistic interpretability, multilingual NLP, and AI safety. I am particularly interested in understanding how internal model representations govern multilingual reasoning and safety behavior.

Previously, I completed my B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence at IIT Hyderabad, graduating first in my department (CGPA 9.34). I also served as a teaching assistant for Convex Optimization.
